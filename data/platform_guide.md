# Product Features

## Login and Account Management

**Learning Portal URL:** [learning.ccbp.in](https://learning.ccbp.in)

### Steps to Access the Portal

1. Open Google Chrome and enter `learning.ccbp.in` in the address bar.
2. Enter the registered mobile number.
3. An OTP will be sent to the registered mobile number and registered email.
4. Enter the OTP to log in.
5. Update profile details accurately, including:

   * Name
   * Year of Graduation
   * Contact Number
   * Other required details
6. After logging in, watch the induction video.
7. Once the video is completed, click **Continue** to unlock the learning schedule.
8. Navigate to:

   **Learning Portal → Home Page → Your Schedule**

   to view the learning plan.

# 1. Home Page

## 1.1 Overview

### Brief Description

The **Home Page** serves as the central dashboard for students upon logging into the Learning Portal. It provides a personalized view that includes greetings, upcoming events, a learning schedule, and key performance indicators such as learning consistency, monthly activity, and leaderboard positions.

## 1.2 Purpose and Design Rationale

### Objective

The primary goal of the Home Page is to offer students a comprehensive overview of their learning journey while enabling quick navigation to courses, events, and other essential modules.

### Design Considerations

The layout is designed for clarity and ease of use. It includes:

* Personalized greetings
* Clearly segmented sections for events, schedules, and performance metrics
* Visual elements such as color-coded labels and progress bars
* An engaging interface that motivates students to stay consistent

## 1.3 How It Works

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Students log into the Learning Portal and land on the Home Page.
2. A personalized greeting, such as **“Welcome, [Student Name]”**, is displayed at the top.
3. An events panel shows upcoming webinars, challenges, and deadlines.
4. The learning schedule section highlights:

   * Scheduled courses
   * Assignments
   * Duration
   * Progress indicators
5. Performance metrics are prominently displayed, including:

   * Learning consistency scores
   * Monthly activity trackers
   * Leaderboard positions
6. Navigation links and buttons provide easy access to modules such as:

   * My Journey
   * Courses
   * Resources

### User Interface Details

* Personalized greeting is placed prominently.
* Distinct sections use color-coded labels and progress bars for quick visual reference.
* Related information is grouped into well-organized panels such as:

  * Events
  * Schedule
  * Performance Metrics

## 1.4 Advantages and Benefits

### Unique Selling Points

* Centralized dashboard that consolidates essential learning information.
* Engaging and visually appealing interface that motivates students.
* Real-time tracking of learning progress and upcoming schedules.

### Benefits for Students

* Saves time by providing a quick overview of the learning journey.
* Enhances motivation and accountability through performance tracking and leaderboards.
* Improves planning and time management by highlighting key events and deadlines.
* Provides personalized learning recommendations based on the student’s learning schedule and course speed.

## 1.5 Usage Scenarios

### Practical Applications

* Students can log in to quickly check their daily learning schedule and upcoming assignments.
* Students can monitor performance metrics to identify progress and areas for improvement.

### Best Practices

* Regularly check the Home Page for updates on events, deadlines, and personal progress.
* Use the navigation links to access detailed course materials and resources efficiently.

# 3. My Journey

## 3.1 Overview

### Brief Description

**My Journey** is a personalized learning path feature that organizes the student’s entire course into **Growth Cycles**. Each Growth Cycle contains multiple topics, assignments, and practice modules, providing a step-by-step roadmap to guide students through their learning journey.

### Note

* There are no quizzes in the My Journey section.
* Once a student completes a Growth Cycle, the next Growth Cycle will be unlocked on the following Monday.

## 3.2 Purpose and Design Rationale

### Objective

The main goal of **My Journey** is to offer students a structured approach to learning, making it easier to understand what to study next and track overall progress.

### Design Considerations

The layout features:

* Visually distinct Growth Cycles
* Progress bars
* Completion percentages
* Pop-ups or tooltips, such as **“What is My Journey?”**, to explain the feature to new users

## 3.3 How It Works

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Navigate to the **My Journey** tab in the Learning Portal’s sidebar.
2. View the list of Growth Cycles, each displaying a progress bar and percentage.
3. Click on a cycle to view detailed:

   * Topics
   * Assignments
   * Projects
4. As tasks are completed, progress updates automatically.
5. Once a cycle is completed, students can unlock additional content or move to the next cycle.
6. A tooltip explains best practices and how to proceed through the cycles.

### User Interface Details

* Progress bars and percentage labels show real-time completion.
* Each Growth Cycle appears as a collapsible card containing related modules.
* Color-coded labels such as **In Progress** and **Completed** help users quickly identify their status.

## 3.4 Advantages and Benefits

### Unique Selling Points

* Clear segmentation of learning materials into manageable cycles.
* Visual progress tracking motivates consistent engagement.
* Tooltip guidance helps new students understand the feature easily.

### Benefits for Students

* Reduces confusion about what to study next.
* Encourages a steady pace by highlighting courses, durations, and deadlines.
* Makes it easier to revisit completed topics for revision.

## 3.5 Usage Scenarios and Examples

### Practical Applications

* A student can quickly see which modules are pending and how much progress has been made in each cycle.
* Success Coaches can guide students to focus on specific cycles when offering support.

### Best Practices

* Check My Journey daily to stay updated on deadlines.
* Complete each cycle before moving to the next for a structured learning flow.

# 3.6 My Journey: Milestone Feature

## Where It Is Located

1. On the left-side navigation panel, click **My Journey**.
2. Inside this section, students can view a breakdown of Growth Cycles and course progress.
3. Milestones appear between Growth Cycles at specific intervals based on completion level.

## What Are Milestones?

**Milestones** are key turning points in a student’s journey that unlock special opportunities such as:

* Internships
* Placement preparation
* Job opportunities

Each Milestone becomes visible once the student completes certain Growth Cycles.

## How Students Can Access Milestones

1. Log in to the CCBP 4.0 Academy portal.
2. Click on the **My Journey** tab in the left-side panel.
3. Scroll through the listed Growth Cycles.
4. View Milestone cards displayed between cycles.
5. If eligible, click the relevant button to access:

   * Internships
   * Preparation materials
   * Jobs

## Why It Is Important

* Guides students step-by-step through learning, internships, and placements.
* Keeps students motivated with visible progress markers.
* Helps students access timely career-building opportunities.
* Encourages learning consistency by connecting opportunities to progress.

# 4. Course Exams and Course Certifications

## 4.1 Course Exams

### Overview

Course Exams are structured assessments designed to help students evaluate their understanding of each course. These exams are based on the content covered and provide an opportunity to regularly check learning progress and build exam confidence.

## 4.2 Purpose and Design Rationale

### Objective

The goal of Course Exams is to offer a consistent method for students to validate what they have learned while promoting discipline and exam readiness through planned assessment schedules.

### Design Considerations

The interface displays:

* Course-wise exam availability
* Past performance
* Exam schedule and guidelines

A strict exam window and guidelines are followed to ensure fairness and a focused exam environment.

## 4.3 How It Works

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Students can access Course Exams from **My Journey**.
2. Course Exams are available at the top-right section.
3. Exams are enabled only on specific days and during scheduled timings.
4. The camera must be enabled throughout the exam.
5. Once the exam is submitted, scores are displayed.
6. If a student does not pass the exam, they can reattempt it on the next scheduled date.

## 4.4 Advantages and Benefits

### Why Course Exams Are Helpful

* Helps students reinforce concepts and prepare better for interviews.
* Builds a habit of regular self-assessment.
* Reduces the pressure of final exams through continuous evaluation.
* Allows students to track performance over time and identify strong and weak areas.

# 4.7 Course Certificate

## Overview

The **Course Certificate** is an automatically issued credential awarded to students upon successful completion of a course in the CCBP 4.0 Academy program. Certificates are sent to the student’s registered email and are also accessible directly from the Learning Portal under the **My Journey** section.

## Eligibility Criteria

To receive a course certificate, students must complete **100% of the course content**, including:

* All learning modules
* All practice exercises
* All assignments
* Other course modules

Partial completion does not qualify for certificate issuance.

## Certificate Issuance Process

* Once a student completes 100% of the course, the certificate is automatically generated.
* The certificate is sent to the registered email address within 24 hours.
* No manual request is required from the student.
* Students can also view and download the certificate from the Learning Portal.

### Navigation Paths

1. **Learning Portal → My Journey → Select Course → More Details → Certificate**
2. **Learning Portal → Profile → My Progress → Certificates**

## Total Certificates Available

A total of **11 course certificates** are awarded up to GC5:

* 10 Technical Certificates
* 1 Non-Technical Certificate: XPM

### Note

Bonus course certificates and the MongoDB certificate are not currently provided.

## List of Certificates from GC1 to GC5

| --: | -------------------------------------- |
|   1 | Build Your Own Static Website          |
|   2 | Build Your Own Responsive Website      |
|   3 | Programming Foundations                |
|   4 | Build Your Own Dynamic Web Application |
|   5 | Introduction to Databases              |

|   7 | Responsive Web Design using Flexbox    |
|   8 | Developer Foundations                  |
|   9 | Node JS                                |

## Troubleshooting and Support Flow

If a student reports not receiving a certificate after course completion, follow these steps:

1. **Verify completion status**
   Confirm that the student has completed 100% of the course, including all modules, exercises, and assignments.

2. **Check email**
   Ask the student to search their registered email inbox, including spam and promotions folders.

3. **Check portal**
   Guide the student to:

   **My Journey → Select Course → More Details → Certificate**

4. **Collect proof**
   If the certificate is still unavailable after 24 hours of completion, ask for a screenshot of the 100% completion status.

5. **Escalate**
   Raise a ticket with the screenshot attached for the concerned team to investigate.

# 6. LastMinute Pro

## 6.1 Overview

### Brief Description

**LastMinute** is a product by NxtWave designed specifically for final-year college students who need comprehensive, last-minute support to secure job placements. It provides a centralized platform offering:

* Off-campus job opportunities
* Company-specific preparation materials
* Important placement courses
* Project-building guides
* AI-powered mock interviews

### Note

LastMinute Pro was part of the old process. Students can now access the **Course Library** to learn topics at their own pace and convenience. For placement guidance and assistance, students can follow the **Academy 2.0 – New Placement Process**.

## 6.2 Purpose and Design Rationale

### Objective

The primary goal of LastMinute is to address the immediate placement needs of final-year students by providing the right tools, resources, and guidance through off-campus drives so they can confidently secure jobs.

### Design Considerations

* **Consolidated Resources:** Combines off-campus drives, mock tests, interview tips, and projects under one platform.
* **User-Centric Approach:** Focuses on quick, targeted revision and practice for final-year students with busy schedules.
* **Scalability:** Offers content for multiple roles and companies, including product-based, service-based, and non-partner companies.

## 6.3 How It Works

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Explore Off-Campus Placement Drives**
   Students can view ongoing, upcoming, and missed drives. They can apply to various job listings from non-partner companies.

2. **Prepare for Companies**
   Students can select specific companies to access:

   * Curated syllabi
   * Important questions
   * Express revision notes
   * Quick practice tests
   * AI-powered interviews through NxtMock

3. **Revise Important Placement Courses**
   Students can access 17 courses covering:

   * Aptitude
   * Programming Languages
   * DSA
   * CS Fundamentals
   * Web Development

   Each course offers top questions, practice sets, and mock tests.

4. **Build Projects**
   Students can choose from up to 9 guided projects, ranging from full-stack applications to Generative AI projects.

5. **Interview Tips**
   Students can learn:

   * Resume-building techniques
   * Project presentation skills
   * Interview do’s and don’ts

6. **AI-Powered Mock Interviews and Assessments**
   Students can use:

   * NxtMock for interview simulations
   * Topin for mock assessments

   These are tailored to selected companies or courses.

### User Interface Details

* The dashboard neatly categorizes:

  * Job listings
  * Preparation modules
  * Courses
  * Projects
  * Interview support resources

## 6.4 Advantages and Benefits

### Unique Selling Points

* Comprehensive coverage of placement needs, from job listings to skill-building.
* Targeted preparation through company-specific modules and AI-driven mock interviews.
* Flexible learning that allows students to choose off-campus drives, revision courses, or projects based on their individual needs.

### Benefits for Students

# 7. Bookmarks

## 7.1 Overview

### Brief Description

**Bookmarks** allow students to save important or interesting coding practice questions and assignment problems for easy access. This feature is not available for sessions, cheat sheets, or other content types.

When a question is bookmarked, it appears in the **Bookmarks** tab on the home screen. This is especially useful for revisiting questions during:

* End-of-cycle revisions
* Exam preparation
* Interview practice

## 7.2 Purpose and Design Rationale

### Objective

The primary goal of Bookmarks is to give students a quick and convenient way to revisit specific coding practice and assignment questions they want to focus on. This helps students strengthen problem-solving skills and prepare for assessments.

### Design Considerations

* A simple bookmark icon appears only on coding questions and assignment pages.
* Sessions and cheat sheets cannot be bookmarked.
* A dedicated Bookmarks tab consolidates all saved items for easy retrieval.

## 7.3 How It Works

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Locate a Coding Question or Assignment**
   While browsing coding practices or assignment problems, students will see a bookmark icon.

2. **Click the Bookmark Icon**
   Once clicked, the question is automatically saved.

3. **Access Bookmarks**
   Students can go to the **Bookmarks** tab in the left navigation menu.

4. **View Saved Questions**
   All bookmarked items appear in a list, allowing quick access for revision or practice.

### User Interface Details

* The Bookmarks tab shows a concise list of saved coding or assignment questions.
* The tooltip **“What is My Bookmarks?”** explains the feature and clarifies that sessions and cheat sheets cannot be bookmarked.

## 7.4 Advantages and Benefits

### Unique Selling Points

* Centralizes frequently revisited or challenging coding questions and assignment problems.
* Enables one-click saving and instant retrieval of important practice questions.

### Benefits for Students

* Useful for end-of-cycle revision and last-minute interview preparation.
* Eliminates the need to repeatedly search through large question banks.
* Encourages focused learning by highlighting key areas of difficulty or interest.

## 7.5 Usage Scenarios and Examples

### Practical Applications

* A student preparing for interviews can bookmark tricky coding problems and practice them repeatedly.
* During a Growth Cycle, students can bookmark challenging assignment questions and review them before moving to the next cycle.

### Best Practices

* Regularly review bookmarked questions to track progress and identify knowledge gaps.
* Remove bookmarks for questions already mastered to keep the list relevant and manageable.
